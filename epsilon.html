Epsilon

Title

The faster epsilon decreases the faster it will stop taking random actions and will follow its data. You could say that if we were to decrease the epsilon value faster the AI would be done training faster. But if epsilon decreases faster it will stop exploring new options sooner. This means that it will stop improving the qValues of the dataset sooner, which will lead to a dataset that is not finished or not very accurate.


So, we can say the slower Epsilon decreases the more accurate our data is. And we can say also say that the faster Epsilon decreases the faster the AI is done learning. For a very simple game such as pong how fast can we decrease the epsilon and still end up with an AI that functions well.


So, let's test what different epsilon values have for effect on the Simple Learning AI.

We will test for 4 different cases:

  1. Epsilon starts at 1 and is divided by 1.001 every update.
  2. Epsilon starts at 1 and is divided by 1.01 every update.
  3. Epsilon starts at 1 and is divided by 1.1 every update.
  4. Epsilon starts at 0 and stays there.
